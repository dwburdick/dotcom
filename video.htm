A long time ago (pictured), I was a comedian. It started in college with sketch comedy, but out in the real world I earned a little spending money by telling jokes at places in Colorado like the Redfish, which is gone now, Jazz At Jack's, which hadn't yet moved into the Pavilions, and some other places that haven't changed all that much.

I hosted two different open-mic shows in Boulder (not concurrently), and I started a variety show called the Secret Circus. That show turned out to be my primary source of income for a year in which journalism didn't treat me very well.

Point being, I've spent a lot of time on stage and in front of cameras delivering all manner of ridiculous dialogue.

Most recently, I hosted a short series of videos for The Denver Post, one example of which you see below.
